About

Welcome to my travel blog, Love Travelling. I’m Megha, traveller writing about my visits to countries near and far. My passion is travel, I just love visiting new places, immersing myself into local cultures of life around the world and discovering the unexpected! Stepping off a plane, I’m always in a hurry to get my passport stamped, reclaim my luggage and walk out of the arrivals hall ready to begin a new adventure. I love the planning, the anticipation, the journey, be it by plane, train.the actual holiday and finally all the wonderful memories and experiences I bring home that live with me forever.

" Travel as much as you can, as far as you can, as long as you can. Life’s not meant to be lived in one place "
